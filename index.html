Zengyi Wo

Tianjin University. College of Intelligence and Computing.

Master's Student

Tianjin University

Tianjin, China

I am a Master’s student at Tianjin University, College of Intelligence and Computing (2022 – 2025), advised by Prof. Wei Wang and Prof. Mingyu Shao. Previously, I received my Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science and Technology from Zhengzhou University (2018 – 2022).

I have collaborated with researchers from Pennsylvania State University and University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign, and completed a research internship at Duxiaoman Finance, working on LLM post-training and domain alignment.

Research Interests

My research interests lie in the intersection of graph learning and large language models, with a focus on:

  • Graph Machine Learning: Graph Neural Networks, Graph Contrastive Learning, Graph Anomaly Detection, Heterophily-aware GNNs
  • Large Language Models: Post-Training (SFT/RLHF), Domain Adaptation, Parameter-Efficient Fine-Tuning
  • Multimodal Foundation Models: Graph-Language Models, Cross-modal Alignment, Multimodal Reasoning
  • Retrieval-Augmented Generation: Knowledge Graph-enhanced RAG, Agentic Reasoning Systems

I have published papers at top-tier conferences such as KDD, WWW, IJCAI, and DASFAA. See my publications page for more details.

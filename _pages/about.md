I am an Algorithm Engineer at **[Baidu](https://www.baidu.com)**, working on **multimodal large language model post-training**. My research focuses on building intelligent systems capable of robust reasoning across modalities, leveraging alignment techniques such as SFT, RLHF, RLVR, and DPO.

Prior to Baidu, I received my M.S. from **[Tianjin University](https://www.tju.edu.cn)** (2025) and B.S. from **[Zhengzhou University](https://www.zzu.edu.cn)** (2022). I was also a Research Intern at **Duxiaoman Finance** (2024).

## Research Interests

- **Multimodal LLM Post-Training:** SFT, RLHF, RLVR, DPO, Reasoning
- **Graph Machine Learning:** GNNs, Contrastive Learning, Anomaly Detection
- **Data Mining:** Heterophily-aware GNNs, Cross-modal Alignment, KG-enhanced RAG

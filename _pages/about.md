I am an Algorithm Engineer at [Baidu](https://www.baidu.com), working on multimodal large language model post-training (2025 – Present). Previously, I received my Master's degree from [Tianjin University](https://www.tju.edu.cn), College of Intelligence and Computing (2022 – 2025), advised by [Prof. Wenjun Wang](https://cic.tju.edu.cn/faculty/wangwenjun) and [Prof. Minglai Shao](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=xxxxx). I received my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Technology from [Zhengzhou University](https://www.zzu.edu.cn) (2018 – 2022).

I have been fortunate to receive guidance from [Prof. Lu Lin](https://ist.psu.edu/people/lulin) (Penn State) and [Prof. Ruijie Wang](https://ruijiewang.github.io) (UIUC). I also completed a research internship at Duxiaoman Finance, working on LLM post-training and domain alignment.

## Research Interests

My research interests lie in the intersection of **graph learning** and **large language models**, with a focus on:

- **Graph Machine Learning**: Graph Neural Networks, Graph Contrastive Learning, Graph Anomaly Detection, Heterophily-aware GNNs
- **Large Language Models**: Post-Training (SFT/RLHF), Domain Adaptation, Parameter-Efficient Fine-Tuning
- **Multimodal Foundation Models**: Graph-Language Models, Cross-modal Alignment, Multimodal Reasoning
- **Retrieval-Augmented Generation**: Knowledge Graph-enhanced RAG, Agentic Reasoning Systems

I have published papers at top-tier conferences such as KDD, WWW, IJCAI, and DASFAA. See my [publications](/publications/) page for more details.

I am a Master's student at [Tianjin University](https://www.tju.edu.cn), College of Intelligence and Computing (2022 – 2025). Previously, I received my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Technology from [Zhengzhou University](https://www.zzu.edu.cn) (2018 – 2022).

I have collaborated with researchers from Pennsylvania State University and University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign, and completed a research internship at Duxiaoman Finance.

## Research Interests

My research interests include:

- **Graph Machine Learning**: GNN, Graph Contrastive Learning, Graph Anomaly Detection
- **Large Language Models**: Post-Training, RLHF, Domain Alignment
- **Multimodal Foundation Models**: Cross-modal Fusion, Graph-Text Alignment
- **Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) & Agents**: Complex Reasoning, Planning

I have published papers at top-tier conferences such as KDD, WWW, IJCAI, and DASFAI. See my [publications](/publications/) page for more details.
